Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. Learn how Cravings collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cravings ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our website, mobile application, and related services. By using Cravings, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Personal details: name, email address, phone number, and login credentials provided during registration.

  • Profile information: saved addresses, profile images, and preferences.

  • Order history: past orders, selected restaurants, and transaction details.

  • Device data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage activity.

  • Location data: real-time location used for delivery and nearby recommendations.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders efficiently and accurately.

  • To personalize your experience with relevant recommendations and offers.

  • To improve our platform performance, features, and usability.

  • To provide customer support and resolve issues or complaints.

  • To send important updates, notifications, and promotional messages.

Sharing Your Information

  • Shared with restaurants to prepare and fulfill your orders.

  • Shared with delivery partners to ensure timely delivery.

  • Shared with payment providers to process secure transactions.

  • Shared with legal authorities if required by law or regulation.

  • We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Cookies & Tracking

  • Cookies are used to remember your preferences and login sessions.

  • Tracking tools help us analyze user behavior and improve services.

  • Cookies enhance browsing experience and website performance.

  • Third-party tools may use cookies for analytics and advertising.

  • You can manage or disable cookies anytime from browser settings.

Data Retention

  • Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide services.

  • We retain information to comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

  • Data may be kept to resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

  • Inactive account data may be removed after a certain period.

  • All data is securely deleted when no longer required.

Your Rights

  • You can access and review your personal information anytime.

  • You can update or correct inaccurate information.

  • You can request deletion of your account and data.

  • You can opt out of promotional emails and notifications.

  • You have the right to raise concerns regarding data usage.
